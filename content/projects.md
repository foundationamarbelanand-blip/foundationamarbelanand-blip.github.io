##### **Free Holistic and Hitech Hospitals:**

We are establishing world-class, completely free hospitals offering not just modern but integrative healing-combining physical core with spiritual well-being. Every patient is seen as a reflection of the Supreme Divine.

##### **Holistic as well as Value Based Hitech Education:**

Education with soul-nurturing young minds in science, spirituality, compassion, and purpose. Our schools and learning centres will offer free, holistic, value-rooted education to all, from early childhood to advance research.

##### **Global Welfare Centres:**

From food, clothing and shelter to counselling, rehabilitation, and livelihood-our welfare centres will support the needy with dignity and divine love, ensuring no soul is left behind as well as free community centres for socio-cultural activities.

##### **Yoga and Divine Realization Programs:**

We offer global programs and retreats guiding seekers towards direct inner connection with the Supreme Vibration (Para-Brahma) through silent Rajayoga Vidya Method, vibration awareness, and divine inspiration-free of any charge.

##### **Divine Media and Motivational Films:**

Creating spiritualy uplifting films, documentaries and digital media that inspire transformation, unity and love-bringing light to hearts and mind across the world.
